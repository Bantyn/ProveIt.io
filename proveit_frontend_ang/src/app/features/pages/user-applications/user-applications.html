Application FAQs

Helpful answers for your submissions

A few common questions based on how applications, submissions, and interviews move inside this platform.

What does "Under Review" mean?

Your application has been received and is currently being checked by the company. No action is needed from your side yet.

When should I submit my project work?

Once your status becomes accepted, submit your project as soon as possible and make sure the link is accessible.

What happens after I become a winner?

Winners usually move to the interview stage, so keep checking this page for scheduled updates and next-step instructions.

Why can’t I see "Submit Work" on every card?

Actions change according to status. Only accepted applications show the submit option, while later stages may show the submitted work link.

How do I prepare for an interview?

Review your project, be ready to explain your approach, and keep your availability clear for the scheduled date and time.

Where can I find updates quickly?

Use the search and status filters at the top of the page to jump directly to accepted, submitted, winner, or interview-scheduled applications.
