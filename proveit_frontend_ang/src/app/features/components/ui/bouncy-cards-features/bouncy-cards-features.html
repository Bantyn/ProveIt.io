Resume-based hiring ​is unreliable

Employers waste time and money filtering candidates who lack the skills they claim.

Fake skills

Candidates freely inflate or fabricate technical proficiencies, creating a false picture of capability.

Slow filtering

HR teams spend hours reviewing hundreds of unverified resumes before a single interview.

Biased screening

Screening on keywords or brand names introduces unconscious bias and misses high-potential candidates

Poor match rate

Without objective skill data, companies make hiring decisions on instinct rather than evidence.
